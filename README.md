# indian-law-training-dataset-2026
Indian Law Training Dataset 2026
Indian Law Training Dataset 2026 is a high-fidelity, structured, and AI-ready repository designed to power the next generation of legal AI systems. It provides comprehensive coverage of the Indian legal landscape, specifically curated for LLM fine-tuning, RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipelines, and automated compliance research.

Key Components
Statutory Frameworks: Digitized collection of Indian legal statutes and central acts.

Judicial Archive: A deep database of Supreme Court judgments spanning from 1950 to 2024.

Landmark Precedents: Curated selection of 373 landmark judgments that defined Indian constitutional and civil law.

Reasoning Benchmarks: Evaluation sets featuring Chain-of-Thought (CoT) legal reasoning for complex advisory use cases.

Use Cases
Legal Research: Accelerating case law discovery and precedent analysis.

Regulatory Compliance: Building automated systems for corporate and MSME legal adherence.

AI Advisory: Training legal bots (like Vakeel GPT) to provide structured, logic-based advisory.

Data Structure
The dataset is organized into AI-optimized formats (CSV/JSON) and includes Jupyter notebooks for direct data processing and model training.
